## An open working group

FoAz as an internet standard is being developed by an alliance of developers from multiple companies, following its inception at [Permit Inc.](https:///www.permit.io)
We invite you as well to join the discussion and collaborate on the future development of the FoAz standard.

## Joining the working group

- Join the conversation at this [Slack workspace](https://io.permit.io/FoAz-community)
- Please direct RFC feedback to [this site's Git repository](https://github.com/permitio/foaz-site/) (Pull requests are welcome)
